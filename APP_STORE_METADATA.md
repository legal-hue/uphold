## Description (4000 chars max)

Something happened at work, at home, or with a contract? Upheld helps you understand where you stand, what your deadlines are, and what to do next.

Upheld is a free legal information tool for people in England and Wales. It covers employment disputes, housing disrepair, contract issues, and creative industry payment disputes.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Tell us what happened. Answer a short set of questions about your situation.
2. See where you stand. Get a clear assessment of your legal position, including strengths, risks, and time limits.
3. Get your plan. Follow a step-by-step guide with practical next actions, template letters, and links to relevant bodies.

WHAT UPHELD COVERS

Employment: Unfair dismissal, discrimination, harassment, unpaid wages, redundancy, settlement agreements, and tribunal deadlines.

Housing: Disrepair, damp, mould, landlord not responding, Section 11 notices, and Housing Ombudsman complaints.

Contracts: Breach of agreement, non-delivery, disputes with businesses or individuals, and letters before action.

Creative: Unpaid invoices, copyright issues, freelancer contract disputes, and small claims guidance.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Upheld provides general legal information, not legal advice. It is not a law firm and using the app does not create a solicitor-client relationship. For advice specific to your situation, consult a qualified solicitor or barrister.

PRIVACY

Your answers and case information are stored on your device only. Upheld does not collect, store, or share your personal data. There are no accounts and no sign-up required.

AI DISCLOSURE

Certain features use artificial intelligence (Claude by Anthropic) to provide legal analysis and draft documents. AI-generated content is for informational purposes only and should always be reviewed before being relied upon.
